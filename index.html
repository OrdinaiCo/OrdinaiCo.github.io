How We Operate

  • Development

    Ordinai development is driven by core values of authenticity, creativity, player engagement, quality, and inclusivity. The team meticulously integrates historical artifacts, encourages innovative approaches, values community input, delivers polished experiences, and fosters a diverse and inclusive gaming environment. These values shape Ordinai into an immersive and engaging RPG for players to enjoy.

  • Values

    The developers of Ordinai, hailing from Lithuania, seek to incorporate values such as authenticity, cultural heritage, inclusivity, collaboration, and community engagement into the project. Drawing inspiration from their own heritage, they aim to create an immersive and respectful game experience that celebrates and reflects aspects of Lithuanian history, while also involving players in shaping the game.

  • Objective

    The primary objective of Ordinai is to create a captivating RPG experience centered around historical artifacts while continuously optimizing and enhancing the game's progression system.
